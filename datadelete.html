User & Data Deletion Instructions
iPaddle English Vocab Builder provides easy access for users to delete their user account and data stored.
  1. Open the iPaddle English Vocab Builder app on your device.
  2. Click the settings icon at the bottom of the app homepage to go to the User Profile page.
  3. Click the "Delete User" button.
  4. Confirm your choice if prompted to proceed with the deletion.
Once you choose to delete your user, all your user data will be automatically deleted permanently.
